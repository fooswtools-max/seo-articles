Is Colosseum Worth It? (2026 Honest Review)

Updated April 2026 · AttractionScout

Is the Colosseum Worth Visiting in 2026?

Verdict: A must-visit. The Colosseum is one of the most remarkable ancient structures on Earth. Walking through the same corridors where gladiators once fought is a visceral, unforgettable experience. The combined ticket with the Roman Forum and Palatine Hill makes it exceptional value.

What Makes It Special

  • Arena Floor: Stand where gladiators fought — the restored platform lets you see the underground passages below
  • Underground (Hypogeum): Walk through the tunnels where animals and gladiators waited — exceptional with a guide
  • Upper Levels: Panoramic views of the arena and across Rome to the Roman Forum
  • Roman Forum + Palatine Hill: Included in every ticket — easily another 2 hours of exploring ancient Rome

Tips to Maximize Your Visit

  1. Book the Full Experience ticket — the underground access is worth every cent
  2. Enter at 8:30 AM — first entry slot is least crowded
  3. Visit the Forum AFTER the Colosseum — most people go there first so afternoons are quieter
  4. Bring water — limited shade, especially on the upper levels

Worth It Because

  • 2,000-year-old engineering marvel
  • Combined ticket = 3 sites for 16 EUR
  • Underground tunnels are extraordinary
  • Free for under 18s

Drawbacks

  • Very crowded midday in summer
  • No shade on upper levels
  • Full Experience tickets sell out weeks ahead
  • Lots of walking on uneven surfaces

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Colosseum worth it with kids?

Yes, children under 18 enter free, and the arena floor and underground tunnels captivate kids' imaginations. Bring snacks and water since options inside are limited. Morning visits work best before heat and crowds peak.

Should I get a guided tour of the Colosseum?

A guided tour significantly enhances the experience, especially for the underground areas where guides share stories you would never get from signs alone. Budget 45-65 EUR for a quality small-group tour including skip-the-line access.

How far in advance should I book Colosseum tickets?

Book at least 2-3 weeks ahead for standard tickets, and 4-6 weeks for Full Experience tickets. Summer months and holidays sell out fastest. Official tickets are available at parcocolosseo.it.

Is the Roman Forum worth visiting too?

Absolutely — it is included in your Colosseum ticket and should not be skipped. The Forum was the center of Roman public life and the ruins are extensive. Allow 1-2 hours to explore properly.
