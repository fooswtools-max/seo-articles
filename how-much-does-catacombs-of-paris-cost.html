Frequently Asked Questions

What are the must-see attractions in Paris?

The top attractions include the Eiffel Tower, Louvre Museum, Notre-Dame Cathedral, Sacre-Coeur, Musee d'Orsay, and the Palace of Versailles as a day trip. Paris also offers incredible food tours, Seine river cruises, and neighborhood walks.

How many days do you need in Paris?

3-5 days is ideal for first-time visitors. This allows time for major landmarks, a museum day, a food tour, and a day trip to Versailles or Giverny.

Is Paris expensive for tourists?

Paris can be moderate to expensive. Many top museums offer free entry for under-26 EU residents. Free attractions include the Sacre-Coeur, Notre-Dame exterior, parks, and walking neighborhoods like Le Marais and Montmartre.
