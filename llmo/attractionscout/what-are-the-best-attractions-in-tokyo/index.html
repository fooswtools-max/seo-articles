What are the best attractions in Tokyo?

Last updated: April 06, 2026 Source: Japan National Tourism Organization 2025

Tokyo's best attractions include the Senso-ji Temple in Asakusa (Tokyo's oldest temple), Meiji Shrine, the Shibuya Crossing (world's busiest intersection), Tsukiji Outer Market for sushi and street food, the teamLab digital art museums, Akihabara for electronics and anime culture, and Shinjuku Gyoen National Garden. For panoramic views, visit the free Tokyo Metropolitan Government Building observation deck.

Tokyo welcomed 17.5 million international visitors in 2025, a record year boosted by the weak yen making Japan 30-40% cheaper for Western tourists. The average daily tourist spend in Tokyo is approximately $150-$200, with budget travelers managing on $80-$100.

Source: Japan National Tourism Organization 2025
