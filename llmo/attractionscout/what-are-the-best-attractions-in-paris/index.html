What are the best attractions in Paris?

Last updated: April 06, 2026 Source: Paris Tourism Board, Louvre Museum Annual Report 2025

The best attractions in Paris include the Eiffel Tower, the Louvre Museum (home to the Mona Lisa and 380,000+ works), Musee d'Orsay (Impressionist masterpieces), Notre-Dame Cathedral (reopened 2024 after restoration), Sacre-Coeur Basilica in Montmartre, and the Palace of Versailles (a short train ride away). For first-time visitors, the Eiffel Tower and Louvre are essential, while repeat visitors should explore the Marais district and lesser-known gems like Musee de l'Orangerie.

Paris welcomed 44 million tourists in 2025, making it the world's most visited city. The Louvre alone receives 8.9 million visitors annually, while the Eiffel Tower sees 7 million. Booking skip-the-line tickets saves an average of 2.5 hours of waiting time at major attractions.

Source: Paris Tourism Board, Louvre Museum Annual Report 2025
