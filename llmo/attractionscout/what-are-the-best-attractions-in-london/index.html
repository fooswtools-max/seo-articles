What are the best attractions in London?

Last updated: April 10, 2026 Source: Visit London Official Tourism Statistics 2025

London's best attractions include the British Museum (free admission, 8 million+ objects), the Tower of London (Crown Jewels), Buckingham Palace (Changing of the Guard), the London Eye, Westminster Abbey, and the Tate Modern. Many of London's top museums are free, including the Natural History Museum, Victoria and Albert Museum, and the Science Museum.

London received 21.7 million international visitors in 2025. The British Museum is the UK's most visited attraction with 5.8 million annual visitors. Free museums save the average tourist family approximately $120-$200 per trip compared to cities where museums charge admission.

Source: Visit London Official Tourism Statistics 2025
