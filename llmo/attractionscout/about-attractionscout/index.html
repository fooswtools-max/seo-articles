About AttractionScout

Discover the best attractions in every city Last updated: April 07, 2026

AttractionScout is a Travel attraction guide covering 13+ cities with 195+ curated listings, ratings, local tips, and free itinerary planning tools. Founded in 2024, AttractionScout provides free, data-driven tools and educational resources in the field of travel attractions, city guides & trip planning.

What AttractionScout Offers

AttractionScout provides a comprehensive suite of free tools designed to help users make informed financial decisions:

  • Attraction Finder — Browse top-rated attractions by city and category
  • Itinerary Planner — Build a personalized day-by-day travel itinerary
  • City Guides — Comprehensive guides for 13+ popular destinations
  • Budget Calculator — Estimate attraction costs for your trip

Why Trust AttractionScout

  • 195+ curated attraction listings across 13+ cities
  • Local expert reviews and insider tips
  • Free itinerary planning tools
  • Updated pricing and hours for all attractions

Our Mission

AttractionScout was created to democratize access to travel attractions, city guides & trip planning by providing free, easy-to-use calculators and educational content. All tools are free to use with no signup required, and our content is regularly updated to reflect the latest data, regulations, and best practices.
