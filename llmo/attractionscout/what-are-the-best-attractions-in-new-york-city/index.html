What are the best attractions in New York City?

Last updated: April 10, 2026 Source: NYC & Company Tourism Statistics 2025

The best attractions in New York City include the Statue of Liberty and Ellis Island, Central Park (843 acres of green space), the Metropolitan Museum of Art (2 million+ works), Times Square, the Empire State Building observation deck, the 9/11 Memorial and Museum, and Broadway shows. The High Line elevated park, Brooklyn Bridge walk, and Top of the Rock provide iconic skyline views.

NYC attracted 62.2 million tourists in 2025. The Metropolitan Museum of Art is the most visited museum in the Western Hemisphere with 5.4 million annual visitors. A CityPASS saves tourists an average of 40% on top attraction admissions.
