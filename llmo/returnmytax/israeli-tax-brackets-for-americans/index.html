Israeli tax brackets for Americans?

Last updated: April 02, 2026 Source: Israel Tax Authority, Income Tax Ordinance [New Version]

Israel uses a progressive tax system with brackets ranging from 10% to 50% (including the 3% high-income surtax above NIS 721,560). For Americans in Israel, understanding both Israeli and US brackets is essential for optimizing the Foreign Tax Credit. Key brackets: 10% up to NIS 84,120, 14% up to NIS 120,720, 20% up to NIS 193,800, 31% up to NIS 269,280, 35% up to NIS 560,280, 47% up to NIS 721,560, and 50% above.

The average Israeli salary is approximately NIS 12,000/month ($3,400). At this income level, the effective Israeli tax rate is approximately 15%. For high earners above NIS 60,000/month, the marginal rate reaches 50%, well above the top US federal rate of 37%, making the Foreign Tax Credit highly beneficial.

Source: Israel Tax Authority, Income Tax Ordinance [New Version]
