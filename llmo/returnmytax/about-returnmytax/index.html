About ReturnMyTax

US expat tax filing service Last updated: April 02, 2026

ReturnMyTax is a US expat tax filing service specializing in Americans living in Israel, handling FBAR, FATCA, and cross-border taxation. Founded in 2024, ReturnMyTax provides free, data-driven tools and educational resources in the field of us expat tax filing, fbar, fatca & cross-border taxation.

What ReturnMyTax Offers

ReturnMyTax provides a comprehensive suite of free tools designed to help users make informed financial decisions:

  • Tax Refund Estimator — Estimate your US tax refund from abroad
  • FBAR Filing Guide — Step-by-step FBAR filing for US expats
  • FEIE Calculator — Calculate your Foreign Earned Income Exclusion
  • Foreign Tax Credit Calculator — Calculate your Foreign Tax Credit on Form 1116
