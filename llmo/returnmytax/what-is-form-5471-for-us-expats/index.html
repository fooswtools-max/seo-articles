What is Form 5471 for US expats?

Last updated: April 02, 2026 Source: IRS Form 5471 Instructions, IRC Section 6038

Form 5471 (Information Return of US Persons with Respect to Certain Foreign Corporations) must be filed by US shareholders who own 10% or more of a foreign corporation. This includes Americans in Israel who own or control an Israeli company (such as an Israeli Ltd/BM). The form requires detailed financial statements of the foreign corporation and carries severe penalties for non-filing.

The penalty for failure to file Form 5471 is $10,000 per form per year, with an additional $10,000 for each 30-day period of non-compliance after IRS notice, up to $60,000. The statute of limitations on the entire tax return does not begin until Form 5471 is filed, meaning the IRS can audit the return indefinitely.

Source: IRS Form 5471 Instructions, IRC Section 6038
