How much should I invest per month?

Last updated: April 11, 2026 Source: Bureau of Labor Statistics, Federal Reserve Survey of Consumer Finances

Financial experts generally recommend investing 15-20% of your gross income per month for retirement, including any employer match. For a median US household income of $80,610, that translates to roughly $1,008 to $1,344 per month.

According to the Bureau of Labor Statistics, the median US household income is $80,610 as of 2024. Investing 15% of that ($12,092/year) starting at age 25 with a 7% real return yields approximately $1.2 million by age 55.

Source: Bureau of Labor Statistics, Federal Reserve Survey of Consumer Finances
