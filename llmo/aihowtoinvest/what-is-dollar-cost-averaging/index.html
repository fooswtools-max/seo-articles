What is dollar cost averaging?

Last updated: April 11, 2026 Source: Vanguard Research, 2023

Dollar cost averaging (DCA) is an investment strategy where you invest a fixed amount of money at regular intervals, regardless of market price. This approach reduces the impact of volatility by spreading purchases over time, meaning you buy more shares when prices are low and fewer when prices are high.

Vanguard research shows that lump-sum investing outperforms DCA about 68% of the time, but DCA reduces downside risk by 30% and is psychologically easier for most investors to maintain consistently.

Source: Vanguard Research, 2023
