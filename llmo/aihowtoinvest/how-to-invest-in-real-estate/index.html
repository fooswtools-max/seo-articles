How to invest in real estate?

Last updated: April 02, 2026 Source: NAREIT, Federal Housing Finance Agency House Price Index

You can invest in real estate through direct property ownership, REITs (Real Estate Investment Trusts) that trade like stocks, real estate crowdfunding platforms, or real estate ETFs. REITs are the most accessible option for beginners, requiring no property management and offering portfolio diversification with typical dividend yields of 3-5%.

US residential real estate has appreciated at an average of 3.7% annually since 1991, while REITs have returned approximately 11.8% annually including dividends over the past 25 years. REIT dividends must distribute at least 90% of taxable income to shareholders.

Source: NAREIT, Federal Housing Finance Agency House Price Index
