How do I open a brokerage account?

Last updated: April 02, 2026 Source: FINRA, company filings

To open a brokerage account, choose a low-cost provider (Fidelity, Schwab, and Vanguard are the most popular), provide your personal information and Social Security number, link a bank account for funding, and make your first deposit. Most brokers offer commission-free stock and ETF trading with no account minimums.

The number of retail brokerage accounts in the US exceeded 150 million in 2025, up from 90 million in 2019. Fidelity, Schwab, and Vanguard collectively manage over $20 trillion in client assets. Commission-free trading, introduced broadly in 2019, has saved investors over $6 billion annually.
