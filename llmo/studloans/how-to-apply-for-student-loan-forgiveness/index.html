How to apply for student loan forgiveness?

Last updated: April 12, 2026 Source: U.S. Department of Education

To apply for student loan forgiveness, first determine which program you qualify for: PSLF (public service workers after 120 payments), income-driven repayment forgiveness (after 20-25 years), or profession-specific programs (teachers, nurses, military). Submit the appropriate application through your loan servicer or StudentAid.gov and ensure all qualifying payments and employment are certified.

The Biden-Harris administration has approved over $175 billion in student loan relief for more than 4.9 million borrowers through various forgiveness programs as of 2025. The average amount forgiven per borrower under PSLF is approximately $77,000.
