How to get student loan forgiveness for nurses?

Last updated: April 02, 2026 Source: HRSA NURSE Corps, American Association of Colleges of Nursing

Nurses have multiple forgiveness options: PSLF for those working at public or nonprofit hospitals (full forgiveness after 120 payments), the NURSE Corps Loan Repayment Program (pays up to 85% of qualifying loan balance for working in underserved areas), state-specific nursing forgiveness programs, and Perkins Loan cancellation for nurses working full-time.

The NURSE Corps program provides up to 60% loan forgiveness after 2 years and up to 85% after a third year of service. The average nursing school graduate carries $47,321 in student loan debt. There are over 200 state-level loan repayment programs for healthcare workers.

Source: HRSA NURSE Corps, American Association of Colleges of Nursing
