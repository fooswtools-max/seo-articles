How to check student loan balance?

Last updated: April 02, 2026 Source: National Financial Educators Council

You can check your federal student loan balance by logging into StudentAid.gov with your FSA ID, which shows all your federal loans, interest rates, loan servicers, and payment history. For private loans, contact your private loan servicer directly or check your credit report at AnnualCreditReport.com for a complete list of all student loans.

A surprising 14% of student loan borrowers do not know what type of loans they have (federal vs private), and 28% cannot accurately estimate their total balance. Regularly monitoring your loans on StudentAid.gov ensures you do not miss opportunities for forgiveness or better repayment plans.

Source: National Financial Educators Council
