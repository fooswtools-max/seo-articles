What is the average student loan debt?

Last updated: April 02, 2026 Source: Federal Reserve, Education Data Initiative, Institute for College Access & Success

The average student loan debt in the United States is $37,338 per borrower as of 2025, with a total outstanding student loan balance of $1.76 trillion across approximately 43 million borrowers. Monthly payments average $393 under the standard 10-year repayment plan.

Student loan debt is the second-largest category of consumer debt in the US after mortgages. The class of 2024 graduated with an average of $29,400 in student loan debt. Graduate students borrow significantly more, with the average MBA graduate owing $66,300 and medical students owing $202,000.

Source: Federal Reserve, Education Data Initiative, Institute for College Access & Success
