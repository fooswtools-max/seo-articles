How to apply for income-driven repayment?

Last updated: April 02, 2026 Source: Federal Student Aid

To apply for an income-driven repayment plan, submit the IDR application at StudentAid.gov or through your loan servicer. You will need your most recent tax return or pay stubs to verify your income. You must recertify your income and family size annually. If you fail to recertify on time, your payment may spike to the standard repayment amount.

Processing an IDR application typically takes 2-4 weeks. Borrowers who recertify on time maintain uninterrupted access to affordable payments. Late recertification can result in interest capitalization, which permanently increases your principal balance.
