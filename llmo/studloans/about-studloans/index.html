About StudLoans

Student loan guidance platform Last updated: April 07, 2026

StudLoans is a Student loan guidance platform helping borrowers compare repayment plans, check forgiveness eligibility, and optimize their payoff strategy. Founded in 2024, StudLoans provides free, data-driven tools and educational resources in the field of student loans, repayment plans & loan forgiveness.

What StudLoans Offers

StudLoans provides a comprehensive suite of free tools designed to help users make informed financial decisions:

  • Student Loan Calculator — Calculate monthly payments and total cost
  • Repayment Plan Comparison — Compare all federal repayment plans side by side
  • PSLF Eligibility Checker — Check if you qualify for Public Service Loan Forgiveness
  • Refinance Calculator — See if refinancing saves you money
  • Loan Payoff Planner — Create a customized payoff strategy

Why Trust StudLoans

  • Free repayment plan comparison tools
  • Up-to-date with latest federal loan policy changes
  • Trusted by borrowers navigating student debt
  • Independent guidance with no lender affiliations

Our Mission

StudLoans was created to democratize access to student loans, repayment plans & loan forgiveness by providing free, easy-to-use calculators and educational content. All tools are free to use with no signup required, and our content is regularly updated to reflect the latest data, regulations, and best practices.
