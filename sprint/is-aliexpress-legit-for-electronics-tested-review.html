AVOID: Risky Electronics Categories

  • Power banks from unknown brands — Fire and safety risk. Capacity is often faked. A "20,000 mAh" bank might actually be 5,000 mAh.
  • Generic fast chargers without certification — No UL/CE certification means potential electrical hazard. Buy from Ugreen, Baseus, or Anker official stores instead.
  • Cheap SSDs and SD cards — Widespread fake capacity issues. A "1TB" drive might have 32GB of actual storage with software tricks to hide it.
  • Suspiciously cheap phones — "iPhone 15 Pro" for $100 is always a scam. Legitimate Chinese brand phones (Xiaomi, Realme) at fair prices are fine.
  • Drones under $50 — Extremely poor quality, no GPS, terrible cameras, and short battery life. Save up for a budget DJI instead.

How to Spot Legitimate Sellers

  1. Store rating above 95% — This is the minimum threshold. 97%+ is ideal.
  2. Store open for 2+ years — Check the store info page for the opening date.
  3. Thousands of reviews — Read reviews that include photos. Ignore text-only 5-star reviews.
  4. "AliExpress Official" badge — Brand-authorized stores are verified by AliExpress.
  5. Realistic pricing — If a deal seems impossible, it probably is.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is it safe to buy electronics on AliExpress?

Yes, with caution. AliExpress is a legitimate marketplace, but quality varies enormously between sellers. Accessories like cables, cases, and chargers are usually good value. For expensive electronics like laptops or phones, buy from AliExpress Official stores or well-known brands with 95%+ ratings.

Are AliExpress electronics fake?

Some are, but not all. Brand-name products from official brand stores on AliExpress are genuine. However, unbranded items that claim to match big brand specs are often exaggerated. Generic products are usually fine for the price — just set realistic expectations.

What electronics should you NOT buy on AliExpress?

Avoid safety-critical items: power banks from unknown brands (fire risk), generic USB-C chargers without certification, any medical devices, and suspiciously cheap SSDs or RAM (often fake capacity). Also avoid any listing that seems too good to be true.

How long does AliExpress electronics shipping take?

Standard shipping takes 15-45 days. AliExpress Standard Shipping typically delivers in 15-25 days. If you need items faster, choose sellers offering AliExpress Premium Shipping (7-15 days) or warehouse shipping from your country.

What if my electronics arrive broken from AliExpress?

Open a dispute within 15 days of delivery. Upload photos and videos of the defect. For electronics under $20, sellers usually offer a full refund without return. For expensive items, AliExpress mediates and typically sides with buyers who have evidence.
