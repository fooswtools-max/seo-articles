AliExpress Direct: The Best Value in 2026

AliExpress Direct is the platform's own logistics network, and it has become the sweet spot for most buyers. It ships from warehouses closer to your country, often includes tracking, and is free or under $2 for most items. Delivery averages 7 to 15 days to the US and 10 to 18 days to Europe.

Look for the "AliExpress Direct" badge on product listings. These items ship faster and have better buyer protection.

Why Free Shipping Is So Slow (And When It Is Worth It)

Free shipping via China Post or Cainiao can take 20 to 50 days because packages travel by sea freight or low-priority air mail. They sit in customs longer because they arrive in bulk shipments.

Free shipping makes sense for items under $5 where you are not in a rush — phone cases, cables, small accessories. For anything you need within a month, pay the $1 to $3 for ePacket or AliExpress Direct.

How to Get the Fastest Delivery

  • Choose AliExpress Direct or ePacket at checkout — the $1-3 premium saves weeks
  • Buy from local warehouses — filter by "Ships from" and select your country or region
  • Order during off-peak times — avoid Chinese New Year (Jan-Feb) and post-11.11 sale backlogs
  • Use AliExpress coupons to offset shipping costs on faster methods
  • Check seller processing time — some sellers take 7 days just to ship

What to Do If Your Order Is Late

Every AliExpress order has a buyer protection deadline. If your order has not arrived by that date, you can open a dispute for a full refund. Before that date, check tracking — most delays happen at customs (3 to 10 days is normal).

If tracking shows "handed over to carrier" for more than 10 days with no update, contact the seller. If they cannot resolve it, AliExpress dispute resolution almost always sides with the buyer.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does AliExpress shipping take in 2026?

Standard shipping takes 15 to 45 days. AliExpress Direct ships in 7 to 15 days. Premium couriers deliver in 5 to 10 business days.

What is the fastest shipping method?

AliExpress Direct and premium couriers (DHL, FedEx) are fastest at 5 to 15 days. AliExpress Direct is often free for qualifying items.

Does AliExpress ship to Europe for free?

Yes, many sellers offer free shipping to Europe. Delivery takes 15 to 30 days via standard methods.

Why is my order taking so long?

Common delays include customs processing, seller processing time, holidays, and high-volume sale periods. Check tracking for the specific hold-up.

Can I get a refund if shipping is late?

Yes. If your order does not arrive by the buyer protection deadline, open a dispute for a full refund.
