Complete guide to AliExpress returns and refunds in 2026. Step-by-step instructions for opening disputes, getting refunds, and returning items successfully.

Understanding AliExpress Returns in 2026

Returning items on AliExpress is different from returning to local stores, but the process is straightforward once you understand it. AliExpress has significantly improved its buyer protection system, and most legitimate disputes are resolved in the buyer's favor.

The key to a successful return or refund is documentation — photos, videos, and clear descriptions of the problem.

Step 1: Check Your Buyer Protection Status

Before opening a dispute, check if your buyer protection period is still active. You have 15 days after delivery confirmation to open a dispute. If the item has not arrived, you can open a dispute after the estimated delivery date passes.

Important: Never click "Confirm Receipt" until you have inspected your item thoroughly. Once you confirm, your dispute window becomes much shorter.

Step 2: Gather Your Evidence

Strong evidence is the single most important factor in winning a dispute. Prepare:

  • Photos showing the defect, wrong item, or damage (at least 3-5 clear photos)
  • Video if the item malfunctions or the problem is hard to show in photos
  • Screenshots of the original listing showing what was promised vs. what you received
  • Measurements if the item is the wrong size

Step 3: Open a Dispute

  1. Go to My Orders in the AliExpress app or website
  2. Find the order and click "Open Dispute"
  3. Select your reason: item not received, not as described, damaged, quality issue, etc.
  4. Choose: Refund Only or Return & Refund
  5. Enter the refund amount you are requesting
  6. Upload your photo and video evidence
  7. Write a clear description of the problem
  8. Submit the dispute

Step 4: Negotiate with the Seller

After you open a dispute, the seller has 5 days to respond. They may:

  • Accept your proposal — You get your refund. Done.
  • Make a counter-offer — They might offer a partial refund. You can accept or reject.
  • Reject your dispute — AliExpress steps in as mediator (Step 5).

Step 5: AliExpress Mediation (If Needed)

If you and the seller cannot agree, click "Escalate Dispute" to involve AliExpress. Their team will review all evidence from both sides and make a binding decision, usually within 7-15 days. Buyers with good evidence win the vast majority of escalated disputes.

Tips for a Smooth Return Process

  • Always open items on camera in case you need video evidence later
  • Keep all packaging until you are satisfied with the item
  • Communicate with the seller through AliExpress messages (not external apps) so there is a record
  • For items under $10, request "refund only" — sellers usually accept rather than paying for return shipping
  • Be polite but firm in your dispute descriptions

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I return an item on AliExpress?

Open the AliExpress app or website, go to My Orders, find the order, and click 'Open Dispute.' Select your reason, upload photo evidence, and choose whether you want a refund only or refund with return. AliExpress will mediate between you and the seller.

How long does an AliExpress refund take?

Once approved, refunds typically take 3-20 business days depending on your payment method. Credit card refunds take 7-14 days, PayPal refunds take 3-5 days, and AliExpress wallet refunds are instant.

Do I have to pay return shipping on AliExpress?

It depends on the reason. If the item is defective, not as described, or counterfeit, the seller should cover return shipping. For change-of-mind returns, you typically pay return shipping, which can cost $15-40 for international returns.

What if the AliExpress seller refuses my refund?

If the seller rejects your dispute, AliExpress will step in as mediator. Provide clear photo and video evidence of the problem. AliExpress almost always sides with buyers who have good documentation.

Can I get a refund without returning the item?

Yes, for items under $5-10 or for defective items where return shipping would cost more than the item, sellers and AliExpress often approve 'refund only' without requiring a return.
