The EU Warehouse Advantage

The fastest way to get AliExpress items in Europe is to buy from sellers with EU warehouses. These items ship from warehouses in Spain, Poland, France, or Germany. Delivery takes 3 to 10 business days instead of 15 to 30. No customs delays. No surprise VAT charges.

To find EU warehouse items: on any product page, look for the "Ships from" dropdown and select a European country. You can also filter search results by "Ships from Europe."

How to Spot a Reliable Seller

  • Over 1,000 orders on the product — proves consistent quality
  • Store age of 2+ years — established sellers are more reliable
  • AliExpress Choice badge — platform-verified for quality and shipping
  • Recent photo reviews — check that real buyers confirm quality
  • Responsive to messages — send a question before ordering big-ticket items

Customs and VAT: What Europeans Need to Know

Since July 2021, all imports to the EU are subject to VAT regardless of value. The good news: AliExpress now collects VAT at checkout for most EU countries. This means your package clears customs faster and you will not get a surprise bill from the postal service.

For orders above 150 EUR, customs duties may apply in addition to VAT. Keep orders under this threshold to avoid extra charges.
