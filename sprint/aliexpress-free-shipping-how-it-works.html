Free Shipping from China — Yes, It Is Real

One of the most common questions about AliExpress is how they can ship items from China for free. It seems impossible — a $2 phone case with free shipping across the world. But it is real, and millions of people take advantage of it every day.

This guide explains exactly how AliExpress free shipping works, what to expect, and how to get the best deals.

How Free Shipping Works

Free shipping on AliExpress exists thanks to several factors:

  • Universal Postal Union subsidies: International postal agreements give Chinese sellers discounted rates for small packages to most countries
  • Seller promotions: Many sellers offer free shipping to attract buyers and boost their ranking on AliExpress
  • AliExpress programs: The platform subsidizes shipping on "Choice" items and during sales events
  • Volume shipping: Large sellers ship thousands of packages daily, getting deeply discounted bulk rates

How to Find Free Shipping Items

  1. Use the "Free Shipping" filter when searching on AliExpress
  2. Look for the "Choice" badge — these items often have free fast shipping
  3. Check the shipping section on the product page before adding to cart
  4. Browse the "Free Shipping" deals section on the AliExpress homepage
  5. Install the AliExpress app — it sometimes offers exclusive free shipping deals

Pro Tip: During major sale events like 11.11 and the Anniversary Sale, AliExpress offers free upgraded shipping on many items that normally charge for fast delivery. Plan your bigger purchases around these events.

Tips for Faster Free Shipping

  • Buy from local warehouses: Many sellers stock popular items in US, EU, and other local warehouses. Filter by "Ships from" to find these.
  • Choose Choice items: AliExpress Choice products typically ship faster and have better tracking.
  • Order early: If you need something by a specific date, order at least 3-4 weeks ahead to be safe with free shipping.
  • Combine orders: Orders over $10 from the same seller sometimes qualify for upgraded shipping at no extra cost.

What About Customs and Import Taxes?

Most small AliExpress purchases clear customs without any additional charges. In the US, packages valued under $800 are duty-free. In the EU, packages under 150 EUR may be subject to VAT, which AliExpress now collects at checkout in many countries. If customs does charge you, it is usually a small amount on higher-value items.

When to Pay for Shipping

Sometimes paying $2-4 for upgraded shipping is worth it. Consider paid shipping when you need the item within 2 weeks, when the product is fragile and needs better handling, or when you want reliable end-to-end tracking. The small upgrade fee is often worthwhile for a much better delivery experience.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does AliExpress really have free shipping?

Yes. Millions of items on AliExpress ship free worldwide. Sellers absorb the shipping cost into the product price or subsidize it through AliExpress programs. Free shipping is available on most items under $20.

How long does AliExpress free shipping take?

Free standard shipping typically takes 15 to 45 days depending on your country. AliExpress Standard Shipping, which is often free on Choice items, delivers in 7 to 15 days. Delivery to the US and Europe is usually faster than to other regions.

Can I track my AliExpress free shipping order?

Most free shipping options include basic tracking. You can track your package on the AliExpress app, or use third-party tracking sites like 17track.net or Parcelsapp for more detailed updates.

Is AliExpress free shipping really free or is it added to the price?

It is a mix of both. Some sellers genuinely offer free shipping as a promotion, while others build a small shipping cost into the product price. Either way, the total cost is still dramatically lower than buying locally.

How can I get faster AliExpress shipping?

Look for items shipped from local warehouses in your country. AliExpress Choice items also tend to have faster shipping. You can also pay a small fee for upgraded shipping methods that deliver in 7-15 days.
