AliExpress Coupons and Deals: How to Save More (2026 Guide)

Master AliExpress coupons and deals in 2026. Learn about store coupons, select coupons, promo codes, sale events, and stacking strategies to maximize savings.

Save Even More on Already Cheap Prices

AliExpress prices are already 50-90% cheaper than local retailers, but with the right coupon strategy, you can save an additional 10-30% on top of that. AliExpress has one of the most generous coupon systems in e-commerce — you just need to know how to use it.

Types of AliExpress Coupons

1. AliExpress Coupons (Platform-Wide)

These work on any item across the entire platform. They are issued by AliExpress itself and typically offer $2-5 off orders over a minimum amount. Find them in the Coupon Center on the homepage.

2. Store Coupons (Seller-Specific)

Individual sellers offer their own coupons that work only in their store. Collect them from the seller's store page before adding items to your cart. Common offers include $1 off $10, $2 off $15, and $3 off $20.

3. Select Coupons

These work across multiple participating stores. They are available during major sale events and can be combined with other coupon types for maximum savings.

4. Promo Codes

Enter these at checkout for additional discounts. AliExpress shares promo codes through email newsletters, the app, social media, and partner websites.

The Coupon Stacking Strategy

Here is how to maximize your savings on a single order:

  1. Collect a store coupon from the seller's page
  2. Get an AliExpress coupon from the Coupon Center
  3. Find a select coupon if available during sale events
  4. Apply a promo code if you have one
  5. Use AliExpress coins for additional cents-off discounts

Example Stack: On a $25 order, you might combine a $2 store coupon + $3 AliExpress coupon + $2 select coupon = $7 off, bringing your total to $18. That is a 28% discount on already-cheap prices.

Daily Savings Tactics

  • Play daily games in the app for free coupon drops and coins
  • Check the flash deals section — limited-time offers with deep discounts
  • Add items to your wishlist and wait for price drops (AliExpress notifies you)
  • Use the price history tool in browser extensions to verify the discount is real

  • Follow your favorite stores — many sellers offer follow discounts of 3-5%

New User Bonuses

If you are new to AliExpress, take advantage of the welcome package:

  • $3 off your first order (no minimum)
  • Additional coupons for the first week worth $5-8
  • Special new-user pricing on featured items
  • Free shipping on many first orders

Some users create a new account to get these bonuses again, but AliExpress tracks devices and addresses, so this is not reliable.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where do I find AliExpress coupon codes?

AliExpress offers coupons directly through the app and website. Check the Coupon Center on the homepage, collect store coupons from seller pages, play daily games for coupon drops, and follow AliExpress on social media for exclusive codes.

Can I use multiple coupons on one AliExpress order?

Yes. AliExpress allows coupon stacking. You can combine one AliExpress platform coupon, one store coupon, and one select coupon on a single order. This is one of the best ways to maximize your savings.

When are the biggest AliExpress sales?

The biggest annual sales are 3.28 Anniversary Sale (March), 6.18 Mid-Year Sale (June), 11.11 Global Shopping Festival (November), and 12.12 Year-End Sale (December). These events offer the deepest discounts of the year.

Do AliExpress coupons expire?

Yes. Most AliExpress coupons have expiration dates ranging from a few days to a few weeks. Store coupons collected from seller pages typically expire within 14-30 days. Always check the expiration date before planning a purchase.

Is there a first-time buyer discount on AliExpress?

Yes. New users get special welcome coupons worth $3-8 off their first orders. These are some of the most generous coupons available and are automatically offered when you create a new account.
