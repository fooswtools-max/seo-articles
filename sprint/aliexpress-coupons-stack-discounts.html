AliExpress Coupons: How to Stack Discounts for Maximum Savings

The AliExpress Coupon Stacking Strategy Most Buyers Miss

Most AliExpress buyers use one coupon and call it a day. Smart buyers stack three types of discounts on a single order to save 30 to 60 percent. AliExpress actually encourages this — the system is designed to let different coupon types combine. Here is exactly how to do it.

The Three Coupon Types (And How They Stack)

All three apply on top of the item's sale price. So if an item is already 40% off, your stacked coupons reduce the discounted price even further.

Step-by-Step: How to Stack for Maximum Savings

Step 1: Collect Store Coupons

Visit the seller's store page and click "Get coupons" or "Store coupons." Most stores offer coupons like "$2 off $20" or "$5 off $50." Follow the store to unlock exclusive follower coupons — these are often better than public ones.

Step 2: Grab AliExpress Platform Coupons

Go to the AliExpress Coupon Center (search "coupon center" in the app). Collect every coupon available — they are free to claim. During sale events, the coupon center has special high-value coupons like "$8 off $60" or "$15 off $100."

Also check: daily coins and games in the app earn you coins redeemable for coupons.

Step 3: Find Promo Codes

Promo codes appear during major sales, through AliExpress social media, and from affiliate partners. Enter them at checkout in the "promo code" field. Common codes during sales: AEAPP03 (app-only discounts), seasonal codes during 11.11, Summer Sale, and Anniversary events.

Step 4: Apply All at Checkout

At checkout, AliExpress automatically applies your best available store coupon and platform coupon. Enter your promo code manually. The order summary shows each discount applied separately. Verify all three are active before paying.

Best Times to Stack Coupons

  • 11.11 Global Shopping Festival — the biggest sale, best coupons, November 11
  • AliExpress Anniversary Sale — late March, excellent platform coupons
  • Summer Sale — June-July, strong discounts on electronics
  • Black Friday / Cyber Monday — late November
  • 12.12 Sale — December 12, last major sale of the year
  • New user bonuses — new accounts get extra coupons for first purchases

Pro Tips for Maximum Savings

  • Use the app, not the website — app-exclusive prices are often 5-10% cheaper
  • Add items to cart early — watch for price drops before sale events
  • Split orders strategically — sometimes two smaller orders with separate coupons save more than one large order
  • Check "Choice" items — these qualify for Select coupons with guaranteed fast shipping
  • Set coupon reminders — coupons expire, and the best ones during sales go fast

Frequently Asked Questions

Can you stack coupons on AliExpress?

Yes. Stack one store coupon, one platform/Select coupon, and one promo code on a single order for 30-60% savings.

How do I get AliExpress coupons?

Coupon center, store pages, app games, new user bonuses, and major sale events.

What is the difference between coupon types?

AliExpress coupons work platform-wide. Store coupons work at one seller. Select coupons work on Choice items. They all stack together.

When are the best sales?

11.11 (November), Anniversary Sale (March), Summer Sale (June-July), Black Friday, and 12.12.

Do coupons expire?

Yes. Most expire within 7-30 days. Check your coupon wallet for dates.
