Is Eiffel Tower Worth It in 2026? Honest Review

Updated April 2026 · AttractionScout

Short answer: Yes, Eiffel Tower is absolutely worth visiting. It is one of Paris's most iconic attractions for good reason. However, there are some tips to make the most of your visit and avoid common disappointments.

Why Eiffel Tower Is Worth It

Iconic iron tower with panoramic city views from three observation levels. This is the kind of experience you simply cannot replicate anywhere else. Whether you are a first-time visitor to Paris or returning for a deeper exploration, Eiffel Tower delivers on its reputation.

What Makes It Special

  • Unique experience: There is nothing quite like Eiffel Tower anywhere in the world
  • Cultural significance: A key part of Paris's history and identity
  • Photo opportunities: Some of the most memorable travel photos you will ever take
  • Educational value: Learn about history, art, and culture in an immersive setting

How Much Does Eiffel Tower Cost?

Standard adult admission to Eiffel Tower is approximately $29. Prices may vary based on the type of ticket (standard vs. skip-the-line), age discounts (students, seniors, children), and seasonal promotions.

  • Adult ticket: ~$29
  • Child/student: ~$14 (varies)
  • Skip-the-line: ~$39-$49
  • Guided tour: ~$54-$69

Tips to Make the Most of Your Visit

  1. Book tickets online in advance — Skip-the-line tickets save 30-60 minutes of waiting
  2. Visit early morning or late afternoon — The smallest crowds are right at opening or 2 hours before closing
  3. Download the official app — Many attractions offer free audio guides via their app
  4. Wear comfortable shoes — You will be walking and standing for 1.5-3 hours
  5. Check for free days — Some attractions offer free entry on certain days or for specific groups

When Eiffel Tower Might Not Be Worth It

  • Extreme peak season — July-August crowds can diminish the experience
  • Without advance tickets — Standing in line for 2+ hours is frustrating
  • Very limited time — If you only have 30 minutes, you will feel rushed

That said, with proper planning (advance tickets, off-peak timing), these drawbacks are easily avoided.

Final Verdict

Eiffel Tower is absolutely worth it and should be on every Paris itinerary. The small investment of $29 delivers an unforgettable experience that you will remember long after your trip.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the must-see attractions in Paris?

The top attractions include the Eiffel Tower, Louvre Museum, Notre-Dame Cathedral, Sacre-Coeur, Musee d'Orsay, and the Palace of Versailles as a day trip. Paris also offers incredible food tours, Seine river cruises, and neighborhood walks.

How many days do you need in Paris?

3-5 days is ideal for first-time visitors. This allows time for major landmarks, a museum day, a food tour, and a day trip to Versailles or Giverny.

Is Paris expensive for tourists?

Paris can be moderate to expensive. Many top museums offer free entry for under-26 EU residents. Free attractions include the Sacre-Coeur, Notre-Dame exterior, parks, and walking neighborhoods like Le Marais and Montmartre.
