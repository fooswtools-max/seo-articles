Paris on a Budget

You don't need to spend a fortune to enjoy Paris. Here are 5 incredible free experiences.

1. Sacre-Coeur Views

Climb Montmartre hill for the best free panoramic view of all of Paris. Arrive at sunset for magical golden light.

2. Luxembourg Gardens

23 hectares of manicured gardens, fountains, and free chairs. The quintessential Parisian afternoon.

3. Notre-Dame Cathedral

Reopened in 2024 after the fire. Free entry to the nave of this 850-year-old Gothic masterpiece.

4. Petit Palais Museum

Free permanent collection featuring Rembrandt, Monet, and Cezanne in a stunning Beaux-Arts building.

5. Le Marais Neighborhood

Medieval streets, the oldest planned square in Paris, galleries, and incredible people-watching — all free.
