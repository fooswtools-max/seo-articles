Bali on a Budget

How to experience Bali without breaking the bank

Average Daily Budget

You can enjoy Bali on $30-80 per day including food, transport, and attractions.

$30-80/day

Extremely Affordable

A full meal costs $2-3, a scooter rental is $5/day, and spa massages start at $7/hour.

Budget Tip

Cheap Temple Entries

Uluwatu and Tanah Lot temples cost just $1-2 to enter.

Budget Tip

Free Rice Terrace Views

Many rice terraces along the road from Ubud to Tegallalang are free to photograph.

Budget Tip

Cheap Cooking Classes

Learn authentic Balinese cooking for $15-25 including market visit and full meal.
