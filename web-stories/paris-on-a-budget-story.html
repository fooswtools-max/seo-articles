Paris on a Budget

How to experience Paris without breaking the bank

Average Daily Budget

You can enjoy Paris on $100-200 per day including food, transport, and attractions.

$100-200/day

Visit Free Museums on First Sundays

Many Paris museums offer free entry on the first Sunday of each month, including the Louvre and Musee d'Orsay.

Budget Tip

Eat Like a Local

Skip tourist restaurants. Grab a baguette, cheese, and wine from a boulangerie for a perfect 5-euro picnic.

Budget Tip

Walk Everywhere

Paris is incredibly walkable. Save on metro tickets by strolling along the Seine and through charming neighborhoods.

Budget Tip

Get the Paris Museum Pass

If visiting 3+ museums, the 2-day pass (55 euros) saves money and lets you skip ticket lines.
