Loan Forgiveness 2026

Multiple programs can eliminate your student loans. Find the right one for your situation.

PSLF

Work for government or nonprofit? After 120 payments (10 years), your remaining balance is forgiven. Tax-free. Over $62 billion forgiven so far.

SAVE Plan

Payments capped at 5% of discretionary income. Interest never grows. Forgiveness after 10-25 years depending on balance.

Teacher Forgiveness

Teach 5 years in a Title I school = up to $17,500 forgiven. Can be stacked with PSLF for even more forgiveness.

IDR Forgiveness

Any borrower on an Income-Driven plan gets remaining balance forgiven after 20-25 years. Currently tax-free through 2025.
