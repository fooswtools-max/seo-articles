Small Claims 101

Someone owes you money? Small claims court is faster, cheaper, and easier than you think. Here's how to win.

Step 1: Demand Letter

Before filing, send a formal demand letter. This resolves ~30% of cases without court. It also shows the judge you tried to settle first.

Step 2: Gather Evidence

Photos, texts, emails, receipts, contracts — organize everything chronologically. Judges love clear timelines. Print physical copies.

Step 3: File Your Claim

Filing fee: $30-75 in most states. Fill out a simple form at your local courthouse. Most courts now allow online filing too.

Step 4: Present Clearly

You have 5-10 minutes. State facts, not emotions. Lead with your strongest evidence. Practice your 2-minute summary beforehand.

Pro Tips

Dress professionally. Arrive early. Bring 3 copies of everything. Address the judge as 'Your Honor.' Be calm and factual.
