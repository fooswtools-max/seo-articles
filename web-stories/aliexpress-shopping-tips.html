AliExpress Like a Pro

AliExpress has amazing deals if you know how to find them. These 5 tips separate great finds from junk.

1. Sort by Orders

Items with 5,000+ orders have been tested by thousands. This is the single best quality indicator. Ignore items with under 100 orders.

2. Read Photo Reviews

Listing photos are often misleading. Click 'Reviews' and filter by 'With Photos.' Real buyer photos show you exactly what arrives.

3. Check Shipping Origin

Filter by 'Ships From' your country. Many items are in local warehouses with 3-5 day delivery. No more 30-day waits.

4. Use the App

AliExpress app prices are often 5-15% cheaper than the website. Plus app-exclusive coupons drop regularly. Always buy through the app.

5. Wait for Sales

11.11 (November), Anniversary Sale (March), and Summer Sale (June) offer 40-70% off. Add items to cart and wait for the next sale.
