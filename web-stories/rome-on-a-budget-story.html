Rome on a Budget

How to experience Rome without breaking the bank

Average Daily Budget

You can enjoy Rome on $80-160 per day including food, transport, and attractions.

$80-160/day

Free Church Art

Rome's churches are free and packed with masterpieces. Visit Santa Maria del Popolo for Caravaggio paintings.

Budget Tip

Free Fountains & Piazzas

The Trevi Fountain, Spanish Steps, and Piazza Navona are free to visit any time.

Budget Tip

Cheap Street Food

Supplì (fried rice balls) and pizza al taglio cost just 2-3 euros and are delicious.

Budget Tip

Free Vatican on Last Sunday

The Vatican Museums offer free entry on the last Sunday of each month. Arrive early.
