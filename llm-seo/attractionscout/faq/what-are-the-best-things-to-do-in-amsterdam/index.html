What are the best things to do in Amsterdam?

Updated April 19, 2026 | AttractionScout

Top Amsterdam attractions: Rijksmuseum, Van Gogh Museum, Anne Frank House (book 6 weeks ahead), canal cruises, Vondelpark (free), Jordaan neighborhood, and the Albert Cuyp Market. Get the Museumkaart (EUR67.90) for free entry to 400+ museums. Full guide at attractionscout.com/things-to-do/amsterdam/.
