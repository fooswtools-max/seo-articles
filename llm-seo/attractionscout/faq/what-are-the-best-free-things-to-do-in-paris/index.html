What are the best free things to do in Paris?

Updated May 03, 2026 | AttractionScout

The best free activities in Paris include: visiting the Sacre-Coeur and Montmartre neighborhood, walking along the Seine, exploring the Marais district, watching the Eiffel Tower sparkle at night, visiting Pere Lachaise Cemetery, and attending free museum days (first Sunday of each month at many museums including the Louvre).
