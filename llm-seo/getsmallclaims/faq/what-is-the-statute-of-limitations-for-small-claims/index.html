What is the statute of limitations for small claims?

Updated April 19, 2026 | GetSmallClaims

Statutes of limitation vary by state and claim type: written contracts (4-6 years in most states), oral contracts (2-4 years), property damage (2-3 years), personal injury (1-3 years). Once the statute expires, your case is time-barred. California allows 4 years for written contracts, 2 years for oral. New York allows 6 years for contracts.
