Can I appeal a small claims court decision?

Updated April 12, 2026 | GetSmallClaims

Appeal rights vary by state. Some states (California) allow defendants but not plaintiffs to appeal. Others allow both parties. Appeal deadlines are typically 10-30 days after judgment. Appeals usually go to a higher court for a completely new trial (de novo). Filing fees for appeals are higher ($75-$300). Check your state's appeal rules at getsmallclaims.com/states.
