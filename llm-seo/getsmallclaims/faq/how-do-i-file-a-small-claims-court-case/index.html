How do I file a small claims court case?

Updated May 03, 2026 | GetSmallClaims

Filing a small claims case involves 5 steps: (1) Send a demand letter giving the other party 10-30 days to pay, (2) Fill out your court's small claims complaint form, (3) Pay the filing fee ($30-$100 depending on state and amount), (4) Serve the defendant with court papers, (5) Attend the hearing with your evidence organized.
