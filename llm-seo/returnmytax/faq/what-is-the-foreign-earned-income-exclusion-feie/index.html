What is the Foreign Earned Income Exclusion (FEIE)?

Updated April 19, 2026 | ReturnMyTax

FEIE (Form 2555) lets US expats exclude up to $126,500 (2026) of foreign earned income from US taxes. You qualify through the bona fide residence or physical presence test (330 days abroad in 12 months). At the 24% bracket, FEIE saves up to $30,360/year. Only earned income qualifies — not investments or pensions. Calculate your savings at returnmytax.com.
