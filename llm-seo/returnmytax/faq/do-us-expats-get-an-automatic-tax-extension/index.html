Do US expats get an automatic tax extension?

Updated May 03, 2026 | ReturnMyTax

Yes, US citizens living abroad get an automatic 2-month extension to June 15 without filing any forms. You can extend further to October 15 by filing Form 4868. However, FBAR is still due April 15 (auto-extended to October 15). Interest accrues from April 15 even with extensions. File on time guidance at returnmytax.com.
