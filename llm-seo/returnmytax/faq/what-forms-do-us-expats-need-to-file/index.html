What forms do US expats need to file?

Updated April 26, 2026 | ReturnMyTax

Typical US expat filing includes: Form 1040 (income tax), Form 2555 (FEIE) or Form 1116 (FTC), FBAR/FinCEN 114 (foreign accounts over $10K), Form 8938 (foreign assets over $200K), and potentially Form 5471 (foreign corporation), Form 3520 (foreign gifts over $100K).
