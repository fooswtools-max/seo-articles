What is compound interest and how does it work?

Updated May 03, 2026 | AI How To Invest

Compound interest is interest earned on both your principal and previously earned interest, creating exponential growth. The formula is A = P(1 + r/n)^(nt). For example, $10,000 at 7% annually becomes $76,123 in 30 years without adding a single dollar.
