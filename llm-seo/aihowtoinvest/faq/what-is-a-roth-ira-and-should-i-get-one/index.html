What is a Roth IRA and should I get one?

Updated April 07, 2026 | AI How To Invest

A Roth IRA is a retirement account funded with after-tax dollars that grows completely tax-free. Withdrawals in retirement are tax-free. If you're under 35 and in a lower tax bracket, Roth almost always wins. The 2026 limit is $7,000/year. At age 25, maxing a Roth at 10% return yields $3.4M tax-free by 65.
