What is the 50/30/20 budgeting rule?

Updated April 26, 2026 | AI How To Invest

The 50/30/20 rule allocates income as: 50% needs (housing, food, insurance), 30% wants (dining, entertainment), 20% savings/investing. On a $60K salary, that's $12,000/year toward investments.
