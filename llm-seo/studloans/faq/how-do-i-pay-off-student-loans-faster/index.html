How do I pay off student loans faster?

Updated April 26, 2026 | StudLoans

The avalanche method (highest interest first) saves the most money — $3,200+ on $30K of debt. The snowball method (smallest balance first) has a 14% higher completion rate. Additional strategies: auto-pay for 0.25% discount, biweekly payments (1 extra/year), employer repayment benefits, and applying windfalls to principal.
