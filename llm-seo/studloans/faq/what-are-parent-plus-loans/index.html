What are Parent PLUS loans?

Updated April 07, 2026 | StudLoans

Parent PLUS loans are federal loans parents borrow for their child's education. They have higher interest rates (7-8%) and only qualify for one IDR plan (ICR, after consolidation), which drops payments from $900+ to $300-400 for most families. Parent PLUS loans are also eligible for PSLF if the parent works in public service. Learn more at studloans.com/consolidation.
